## 2 Country Brief

Cabo Verde, a stunning island nation off West Africa’s coast, is known for its stable democracy, volcanic landscapes, and upbeat cultural vibe. While small in population, the country punches above its weight in renewable energy, ocean tech, and digital governance. With expanding fiber connectivity, relaxed visa rules, and a welcoming attitude, Cabo Verde presents a peaceful base for remote developers and small tech entrepreneurs targeting Africa or Europe.

## 3 Country Attractiveness Overview

### a. Safety and Security

- One of West Africa’s safest nations
- Low political unrest and minimal violent crime
- Friendly for tourists and expats alike

### b. Healthcare Quality

- Basic but improving healthcare infrastructure
- Public and private clinics in Praia and Mindelo
- Medical evacuations to Portugal for complex care

### c. Education Quality

- Portuguese-based system, emerging tech programs
- University of Cabo Verde offers ICT and engineering degrees
- Tech literacy improving via EU and UNDP initiatives

### d. Economic Stability and Employment

- Tourism, fisheries, and remittances are key sectors
- Government promoting digital economy through “Digital Cabo Verde” strategy
- Growing role in blue economy and solar innovation

### e. Infrastructure

- Strong inter-island ferry and air connectivity
- Internet improving with recent fiber expansion
- High mobile penetration and rising fintech adoption

### f. Environmental Quality

- Clean air, scenic coasts, and low pollution
- Volcanic peaks and white beaches coexist beautifully
- High potential for eco-conscious digital nomads

### g. Cost of Living

- Moderate – lower than most of Europe
- Groceries, rent, and transit affordable
- Imported electronics and goods more expensive

### h. Social and Cultural Environment

- Diverse Afro-Portuguese culture with warm hospitality
- Indian cuisine and culture not common, but welcomed
- Easy lifestyle, slower pace — ideal for focused remote work

### i. Access to Nature and Recreation

- Great for diving, surfing, and island hopping
- Volcanic hiking trails and serene beach escapes
- Strong live music and café culture in cities

### j. Governance and Civic Services

- E-governance and digital ID systems in place
- High press freedom and transparent elections
- Efficient bureaucracy relative to region

## 4 Pathways & Opportunities

### a. Admissions to Top Tech Universities

- **University:** Universidade de Cabo Verde
- **Programs:** IT, Networks, Marine Technology
- **Tuition Fees:** ~$400–$800/year
- **Post-Study Options:** Remote jobs or local digital startups

### b. Top Local Tech/IT Companies

- **ISPs:** CVTelecom, Unitel T+
- **Digital Startups:** Blue Oasis, Prime Consulting
- **Incubators:** Prime Digital Hub, Nest CV

### c. Immigration / Work Visas for Indian Tech Professionals

- **Work/Residence Permit:** Sponsored by company or freelance declaration
- **Startup Visa:** Encouraged for foreign founders
- **Digital Nomad Visa:** Being explored for remote professionals

### d. Remote Software Development Jobs from India

- Euro-compatible time zone makes async feasible
- Use platforms like: **LinkedIn**, **Remote OK**, **AngelList**, **EU job boards**
